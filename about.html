The NoShooga Story

Long Story Short

NoShooga is a Berkeley-based kitchen endeavoring to ensure we can have our cake and eat healthy too.

Using only fruit, seeds, and spices, NoShooga offers an array of snacks and delectable treats that are naturally sweet without any added sweeteners and also vegan and free of common allergens. All NoShooga snacks are made by hand by someone who really loves snacks, skates, and capes.



Meet Danika

Hi, I'm Danika. When people ask me why I make NoShooga snacks my reply is always, "You Gotta Have Snacks!".

Having sustainable quality calories to go is crucial. What is more is that NoShooga will satisfy your dessert desires and will also make a healthy and balanced breakfast too. It's awesome to jet through the day satiated without having to stop for snacks. My day is transformed with great snacks, and yours will be too.
